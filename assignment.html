PANEER BUTTER MASALA Reciepe

Paneer Butter Masala is one of India's most popular paneer gravy recipe. This recipe with Indian cottage cheese cubes in a creamy tomato sauce is one of the reciepe which you can find everywhere throughout the India. With my step-by-step guide you can easily make this restaurant style paneer butter masala at home!. This creamy curry is made with onion, tomatoes, cashews, cream and butter (of course!). It has a mild sweet taste and pairs beautifully with garlic naan or jeera rice.

Tips to Make the Best Paneer Butter Masala

to know the tips click here

Okay, I have heard from people so many times-that homemade paneer butter masala doesn't taste like the one at restaurants.

Here's what you need to do to make it absolutely perfect!

Use nuts in the gravy: yes, it makes the sauce thick and creamy and so you need to use nuts in the recipe.

Cashews and almonds both work, you can even use a combination of both.

However, do not use a lot of nuts because then the final curry will be super thick and I wouldn't recommend that.

I suggest using 10-15 cashews for 250 grams of paneer.

Be generous with cream and butter: I am not asking you to use 1 cup of both but don't use 1/2 teaspoon either!

This is an indulgent dish and so you need to little generous with fat if you are looking for that restaurant style taste.

Use minimal spices: this recipe doesn't need a lot of spices. A bit of garam masala and cardamom powder is plenty.

I also add little chili powder and Kashmiri red chili powder (mainly for the color).

The flavors of this dish come from fresh tomatoes, cashews, ginger, garlic, butter and cream!

    HOW TO MAKE GRAVY BASE

    The steps to be Followed are given below.....

    1. Firstly, in a large kadai heat 2 tbsp oil and add 2 tbsp butter.

    2. Add 5 cloves, 2 pods cardamon and saute on low flameuntil it turns aromatic.

    3. Now add 2 onions, 2 clove garlic,1 inch ginger.

    4. Saute until onions shrink slightly.

    5. Further add 3 Tomato(sliced), 15 Cashew and saute for 2 minutes.

    6. Cover and boil for 10 minutes, or until tomato softens.

    7. Cool completely and transfer to the blender and blend to smooth paste.

    8. Pass the curry to the filter to get rid of seeds and skin.

    9. Smooth and Silky, the Tomato-Onion paste is ready. No wkeep aside.

    HOW TO MAKE PANEER BUTTER MASALA

    The steps to be Followed are given below.....

    1. Firstly, in a large kadai heat 2 tbsp oil, 2 tbsp butter, 1 bay leaf and 1 chilli, saute until the spices turn aromatic.

    2. Now add 1 Onion and saute until onions turn to golden brown.

    3. Keeping the flame on low, add 1/2 tsp turmeric, 1 tsp chilli powder, 3/4 tsp coriander powder, 1/4 tsp cumin powder, 1/2 garam masala and 1 tsp salt.

    4. Saute until the spices turn aromatic. Make sure not to burn the spices.

    5. Add in prepared Onion Tomato paste and mix well.

    6. Cook until the oil seperates from the Masala paste.

    7. Now add 1 cup water and mix well adjusting the consistency.

    8. Further add in 2 tbsp Cream and mix well.

    9. Also, add 20 cubes of paneer and mix gently.

    10. Simmer for 5 minutes, or until the panner absorbs the flavour.

    11. Add 1 tsp Kasuri Methi and 2 tbsp Coriander, Mix Well.

    12. Finally, enjoy PANEER BUTTER MASALA with Roti or Rice or Naan.
